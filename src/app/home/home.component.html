    About Vibha Corporation & Co

    Vibha Corporation & Co Vibha is the name of Goddess Mahalakshmi. Vibha refers to Shining Bright Light. Vibha Corporation will operate in the Field of Agriculture Food Education Real Estate & Construction Solar Energy Vision Qualitative Vibha will integrate the strength and core-competencies of all natural and identified resources to ensure consistent region level growth in the Field of Agriculture – Natural Way Farming, Food – Ready to Eat & Ready to Cook Chapathis, Guidance, Support, Life & Soft Skill, Core Area & Entrepreneurial Training, Real Estate Development & Construction and Solar Energy Quantitative Natural Way Farming: To Cultivate in Excess of 400,000 Acres of Land in Tamil Nadu Food: To Achieve Daily Sales of 1 (One) Million Chapathis Education / Coaching / Training: To Guide, Train, Empower and Employ 100,000 Talent(s) by 2030 Real Estate and Construction: To Develop, Construct & Deliver Two (2) Million Sq. Ft of Residential & Commercial Space in Chennai and Bangalore by 2030 Solar Energy: To install and harvest 5 GW of Roof Top Solar Power Mission To adhere to the best ethical and traditional practices that would deliver excellence through our committed, well – trained smart workforce and be responsible for growth to all stake holders. Partners Sundar Rajan Jagannathan (SRJ) – Managing Partner SRJ was the Head of Radiance Realty Developers India Limited as its Chief Operating Officer between July 2010 to Jan 2013 in Chennai and June 2015 to May 2021 in Bangalore. Prior to that, SRJ was the Head of Land (Investments, Development & Industrial) Services division at CB Richard Ellis Chennai Transaction Management Group. SRJ responsibilities in development side included Sourcing of Land – Acquisitions – Project Definition – Government Co-ordination for Approvals – Product Segmentation – Pricing Strategy – Sales & Marketing Planning & Scheduling – Financial Closure – Bank Co-ordination – Documentation – Project Level Co-ordination / Clearances (Management & Client Perspective(s)) – Client Servicing & Management – Handing Over Etc., In CB Richard Ellis, SRJ responsibilities were primarily clients-directed and comprehensive. His skill-set included understanding Diversified Needs of his Clients and perform Transaction Management – Land / Space Acquisition and Leasing, Industrial Space Acquisitions and Dispositions. In all, SRJ has handled projects in excess of INR 1200 Crores. He had devoted lot of time & effort to strengthen the CBRE’s strategic development of the Investments and Financial Service Line to represent Project Developments (Residential & Commercial) in South India. Over the Years, due to his Diversified Operational Experience, SRJ has developed keen interest in the field of Agriculture, Food, Solar Energy, Education and needless to mention – Real Estate & Construction Operations. Vibha Corporation is the brainchild of SRJ. Vibha will excell in the above chosen areas over the next 2 decades. Kanakavalli Sundar Rajan (KSR) KSR is Mrs. SRJ and having understood the ambitions of her better-half, she has extended her helping arm in all possible ways to SRJ. She holds a Bachelor of Business Administration (B.B.A) Degree from Vidyasagar College of Arts & Science, Chengelpet. She is a Home – maker cum Partner in all the ventures. She hails from Singaperumal Koil near Chengalpet. She has been the pillar to SRJ in all his initiatives and works till date personally and professionally.
